Flazz BCA: Your Mobile Banking Solution

What is Flazz BCA?

Flazz BCA is a digital payment and banking solution offered by PT Bank Central Asia Tbk. (BCA). It provides a convenient way to manage your finances and make payments using a single card. Whether you're a student, a staff member, or an alumnus of BINUS University, Flazz BCA is designed to simplify your financial transactions.

Why Did BCA Create Flazz?

BCA introduced Flazz as a versatile and convenient contactless payment solution to meet the growing demand for efficient digital payment methods in Indonesia. Flazz serves as an inclusive, secure, and fast payment option for various transactions, including public transportation, retail purchases, and more, promoting financial inclusion and reducing the reliance on cash. BCA's initiative aims to advance digital payment adoption, enhance efficiency for businesses, and bolster security, resulting in a widely accepted and popular payment method in the country.
