Top Up Your Flazz BCA Card

  • Steps to Top Up Your Flazz BCA Card at an ATM

    1. Insert your Flazz BCA card into the ATM machine.
    2. Follow the on-screen instructions to select "Top-Up" or "Prepaid Card Reload."
    3. Enter the desired top-up amount and confirm the transaction.
    4. Collect your card and transaction receipt.

  • Steps to Top Up Your Flazz BCA Card via Mobile App

    1. Open the BCA mobile app.
    2. Log in to your account.
    3. Select the "Top-Up Flazz" option.
    4. Enter your Flazz card number and the top-up amount.
    5. Confirm the top-up transaction.

  • Steps to Top Up Your Flazz BCA Card at a Merchant Shop

    1. Visit a participating Flazz merchant (e.g., convenience stores).
    2. Ask the cashier for a Flazz top-up.
    3. Provide your Flazz card to the cashier.
    4. Specify the top-up amount.
    5. Pay the cashier, and your Flazz card will be topped up.

  • Steps to Top Up Your Flazz BCA Card at a Nearby Branch

    1. Visit a BCA bank branch near you.
    2. Queue at the customer service desk.
    3. Provide your Flazz card and inform the staff that you want to top up your card.
    4. Specify the top-up amount and complete the payment.
    5. Your Flazz card will be topped up at the bank branch.
